I've been dancing since 2006, when I started attending salsa classes at Caltech. I've tried many partner dance styles to varying extents, including salsa, Argentine tango, ceroc, hustle, and lindy hop. Now, I mainly do West Coast swing and International ballroom and Latin.

If you are interested in learning more about dance and about social dancing venues and dance stores in Southern California, read on to the list of links I've compiled.

Dance Links

Online References

  • ballroomdancers.com – Videos of basic syllabus figures in American, International, and nightclub dances. Basic bronze videos are free. More videos are available with paid memberships. Also has discussion forums and partner search.
  • Dance Forums – Discussion forums about many styles of dance, including ballroom, swing, tango, and salsa.

Dancing in the Los Angeles and Orange County Area

  • LindyGroove – Weekly Thursday night lindy hop dance at the Masonic Temple in Pasadena.
  • Westie Seconds – West Coast swing on the second Saturday of every month at the Fred Astaire studio in Pasadena.
  • Latin Dance Pros – Monthly ballroom and salsa parties at the Sonata Ballroom in Arcadia.
  • Swingin' the Blues – Weekly Thursday night West Coast swing dance at Atomic Ballroom Irvine.
  • Swingin' the Club – Weekly Tuesday night West Coast swing dance at the Hacienda Hotel in El Segundo.
  • Orange County West Coast Swing Dance Club – Weekly Sunday night West Coast swing dance at Atomic Ballroom Placentia. Jack and Jill contests are held on the first and third Sundays of every month. An OCWCSD membership is required to compete in the contests.

Dance Shoes

  • Very Fine Dance Shoes – Affordably priced standard, smooth, Latin, rhythm, salsa, swing dance shoes. Buy online or at the warehouse in El Monte.
  • Champion Dance Shoes – Sells imported dance shoes. Located in Los Angeles. I accidentally discovered their store while on a bus ride to the Hollywood Bowl. I've never been inside.
